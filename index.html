Our Story

Crafted with
Intention

Sohima Production was born from a singular belief — that stories are the most powerful force in the universe. We give them form, voice, and motion.

From the first word on a page to the last frame of an animation, we obsess over craft. Our team of writers, narrators, directors, and animators collaborate to produce works that don't just entertain — they stay with you.

Whether it's a debut novel, a resonant audiobook, or a breathtaking animated world, every project receives the same commitment: uncompromising creative excellence.

Founder

Meet the Owner
Founder & Creative Lead

The owner of Sohima Production is a storyteller, curator, and creative producer with a deep belief in the transformative power of narrative. They lead every project with passion, precision, and a commitment to cinematic storytelling.

With experience across books, audiobooks, and animation, they bridge creative vision and delivery, ensuring each production stays true to its voice while achieving polished, professional results.

Driven by the desire to craft enduring stories, they guide the team with an emphasis on emotional resonance, artistic integrity, and audience connection.

Co-Founder

Meet the Co-Founder
Vocalist & Creative Collaborator

The co-founder of Sohima Production brings her voice and instinct to every project she touches. As a vocalist, she has lent her talent to the studio's music ventures, adding warmth and soul to the sound.

Beyond music, she plays an active creative role — offering ideas, feedback, and perspective that shape the direction of projects across the studio.

Her support extends far beyond the credit she takes — steady, encouraging, and always present, she is as much a part of Sohima Production's story as any title could describe.

How We Work

The Process

01

Discovery

We begin by listening. Understanding your vision, your voice, and the story you are trying to tell is the most important step of any project.

02

Development

Our creative team collaborates with you to develop the project — structuring the narrative, building the aesthetic, and defining the production plan.

03

Production

With a clear direction set, we execute with precision — writing, recording, designing, and animating — with you involved at every milestone.

04

Launch

We deliver a finished work ready for the world — polished, formatted, and supported through release across all relevant platforms and markets.
